RANJ

Feature Film | 97 Min Approx | Punjabi w English Subtitles

‘Ranj’ is a story of a loner, Amanpreet, who is driven to despair in a hostile big city. On the one hand, the film tells a simple human tale of an individual’s re-awakening following his moralistic dilemma; and on the other, it questions the notion of the modern-day progress, where materialistic growth has been hopelessly allowed to preside over social quietude.
